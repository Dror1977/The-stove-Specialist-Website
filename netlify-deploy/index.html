About Us

Meet The Stove Specialist Team

We are a Sydney based company servicing the Sydney Metro. Our tradesmen have extensive experience and are qualified to assist you with almost all your home appliance needs.

Our vision is to supply a comprehensive service in a timely and cost effective way. We have specialists in all home appliances which enables us to provide solutions and options for almost any problem.

The Stove Specialist evolved into what it is today, when the owner of the Stove Specialist identified a need for a single trade company that connected all the trades through a central point, knowing some jobs require more areas of expertise to ensure the right solution is delivered the first time.

Sydney's Electric & Induction Cooktop Experts

Is your electric or induction cooktop not heating properly? Elements malfunctioning? Touch controls not responding? Our expert Electric Cooktop Specialists understand the advanced technology behind modern electric and induction cooking systems. We provide comprehensive repair and installation services using cutting-edge diagnostic tools and genuine parts.

Understanding Induction Technology

Induction cooktops use electromagnetic energy to create heat directly in compatible cookware. A metal coil under the cooking surface generates a magnetic field that induces electrical currents in ferromagnetic pots and pans, converting them into their own heat source.

Key Benefits:

  • • 40% faster heating than gas
  • • 90% energy efficiency
  • • Precise temperature control
  • • Safer cooking surface

Common Features:

  • • Pan size detection
  • • Auto presets (melt, simmer, boil)
  • • Touch controls
  • • Safety locks
